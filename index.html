📖 Print Booklet Guide

Recommended printer settings for perfect results:

  • Paper Size: Match your chosen output (A4/Letter)
  • Orientation: Landscape
  • Print on both sides: Yes (Duplex)
  • Flip on: Short edge
  • Scaling: 100% (no scaling)
  • Pages per sheet: 1
  • Binding: Saddle stitch (for folded booklets)

After printing, fold all sheets in half and staple along the spine for a perfect booklet.
